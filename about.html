About Tinaye Electro-Mechanical

Leading material handling solutions since 1998

Our Story

Tinaye Electro-Mechanical (Pvt) Ltd T/A Limit Link Services is a registered company situated at 10 Nicholson Road, Romney Park, Bulawayo. We are a private limited company incorporated in Zimbabwe under the Zimbabwe Companies Act (Chapter 24:03) with registration number 269/2017.

For over two decades, we have specialized in the material handling industry, providing inspection, training, and equipment solutions that ensure safety, compliance, and operational excellence across Zimbabwe.

Our Mission

"To provide a safe working environment to the business community by ensuring reliability, safety and compliance through inspection and training."

Our Vision

"To be the world leader in providing material handling solutions and height safety assurance."
